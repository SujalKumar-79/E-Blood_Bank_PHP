Simple Process

How it works

From registration to saving a life — it takes just a few steps.

💉 For Donors
1

Register Free

Create your donor profile with personal and medical information in under 5 minutes.

2

Get Verified

Our team reviews your eligibility. Approved donors are marked as active within 24 hours.

3

Receive Alerts

Get notified when your blood type is needed near you. Accept or decline at your discretion.

4

Donate & Save

Visit the designated hospital or blood bank. Log your donation and earn a digital certificate.

🏥 For Requesters
1

Register Account

Create a requester profile with your patient and hospital information.

2

Submit Request

Specify the blood type, units needed, urgency level, and hospital location.

3

Match Found

Our system instantly matches you with available verified donors nearby.

4

Receive Blood

Coordinate with the matched donor or bank and track the delivery in real time.
